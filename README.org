* Keyboard/Mouse shortcuts
** SPACE
Show hints
** [1-9]
Switch to the page by index
** [a-z]
Open link by index
** /
Focuses the seachbar
** ?
Switch to Searcher
** Right-click, - (MinusKey)
Toggle Edit mode
** + (PlusKey), = (EqualKey)
Add new site
** . (PeriodKey)
Open options
** , (CommaKey)
Focus theme switcher
** Scroll Up/down
Switches page Up/down
